Lasagna

Description

An easy lasagna recipe that saves me lots of time. This dish satisfies everyone in the family. Try it tonight!

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Beef Mince
  • Pasta Sheets
  • Tomato Paste and Puree
  • Salt, Pepper and dried parsley to taste

Steps

  1. Boil water in a pot
  2. Add pasta sheets
  3. Brown mincemeat
  4. Prepare pasta sauce
  5. Salt to taste and serve accordingly
